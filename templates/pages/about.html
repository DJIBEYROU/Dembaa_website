Welcome to GLOBELINX TRADING LIMITED

GLOBELINX TRADING LIMITED is a leading worldwide used car exporting company based in Japan, specializing in Japanese vehicle brands such as Toyota, Nissan, Honda, Mitsubishi, Mazda, and more. With our unwavering commitment to exceptional service and quality vehicles, we ensure customer satisfaction and peace of mind throughout the entire buying process. From sourcing to inspection and export, our expertise in Japanese vehicles sets us apart, making us the trusted choice for customers worldwide."

Export Compliance

We ensure that all our export processes comply with international trade regulations and export laws. By adhering to these regulations, we prioritize ethical and legal practices in our operations. This commitment to compliance gives customers confidence in our company's integrity and ensures a smooth and hassle-free export experience.

Shipping Arrangements

We take care of organizing the shipping logistics for our customers. This includes selecting reliable and reputable shipping partners, negotiating competitive rates, and arranging for the safe transportation of vehicles to their destination ports. We provide customers with updates on shipping schedules and tracking information, so they can stay informed about the progress of their vehicle's journey.

Insurance Coverage

We understand the importance of protecting our customers' investments. That's why we offer insurance coverage options for vehicles during the shipping process. This provides customers with added peace of mind, knowing that their vehicle is protected against any unforeseen events or damage that may occur during transit.

Timely Delivery

We understand that our customers are eager to receive their purchased vehicles. We strive for timely delivery, coordinating efficient shipping routes and closely monitoring the shipping process. While delivery times can vary depending on the destination and shipping method chosen, we make every effort to ensure prompt and reliable delivery of vehicles to our customers' designated locations.

Efficient Documentation

As an experienced used car exporting company, we have a streamlined process for handling all the necessary documentation involved in exporting vehicles. Our team of experts will guide customers through the paperwork, ensuring compliance with international regulations, customs requirements, and import/export documentation.

Customs Clearance

We understand that navigating customs clearance can be complex and time-consuming. With our knowledge and expertise, we handle the customs clearance process on behalf of our customers. This includes preparing all the required documents, coordinating with customs authorities, and ensuring smooth clearance of the vehicle for export.

Destination Support

Once the vehicle reaches its destination port, we can provide assistance in coordinating local services if needed. This includes connecting customers with trusted local partners for services like customs clearance, transportation, and registration in their respective countries. Our goal is to support customers throughout the entire import process, ensuring a seamless transition from port to road.
