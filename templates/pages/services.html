Our Services

The steps below provide an explanation of our services.

1

PHASE 1. ORDER

Select the car you want to buy through our inventory. This operation can be done through our vehicle search engine. Also, you can optimize your search accordingly.

2

PHASE 2. CONFIRM ORDER

Check vehicle specifications, photos, and inspection sheets to know the real vehicle condition. Set your purchase conditions, for example, insurance, inspection, destination country and port of discharge, and get the final price.

3

PHASE 3. CONFIRM PAYMENT (BANK WIRE TRANSFER or PAYPAL)

Confirm payment for the selected vehicle as per the invoice generated from our system in our Japan Bank Account (link to bank details). All payments should be sent ONLY to GLOBELINX TRADING LLC beneficiary account in JAPAN, and you will be notified by email after confirming your payment in our account.

4

PHASE 4. SHIPMENT AND DOCUMENTS

Arrange the earliest shipment available. All necessary documents will be couriered directly to you or your agent a week after departure.

5

PHASE 5. CUSTOM CLEARANCE & VEHICLE REGISTRATION

Contact your local clearing agent to complete all import procedures and assist you with taxes and other charges to be paid. Receive, get the vehicle registered, and enjoy driving your GLOBELINX TRADING vehicle.

7

PHASE 6. Vehicle Delivery

Get in your car and enjoy the ride.
