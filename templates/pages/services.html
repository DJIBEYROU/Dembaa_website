Our Services

The steps below provide an explanation of our services.

1

PHASE 1. ORDER

Select the car you want to buy through our inventory. This operation can be done through our vehicle search engine. Also, you can optimize your search accordingly.

2

PHASE 2. CONFIRM ORDER

Check vehicle specifications, photos, and inspection sheets to know the real vehicle condition. Set your purchase conditions (destination country and port of discharge).

3

PHASE 3. PROFORMA INVOICE

Receive Proforma Invoice for the selected vehicle please note that all payments should be sent ONLY to GLOBELINX TRADING LLC beneficiary account in JAPAN.

4

PHASE 4. CONFIRM PAYMENT

Confirm payment for the selected vehicle as per the proforma invoice and you will be notified by email after confirming your payment in GLOBELINX TRADING LLC account in Japan.

5

PHASE 5. SHIPMENT & DOCUMENTS

GLOBELINX TRADING LLC will arrange the earliest shipment available. All necessary documents will be couriered directly to you or your agent a week after departure.

6

PHASE 6. CUSTOM CLEARANCE

Contact your local clearing agent to complete all import procedures. If you need assistance about these procedures, feel free to contact GLOBELINX TRADING LLC main office in Japan.

7

PHASE 7. VEHICLE REGISTRATION

Get the vehicle registered, apply for insurance, and enjoy driving your GLOBELINX TRADING vehicle with safety and comfort.
